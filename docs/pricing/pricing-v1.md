# Pricing v1 — RSP AI Editor

## Plan Matrix

### Free ($0)
- 5 image edits/day
- 10 copy rewrites/day
- Standard export quality
- Session history (24h)
- Watermark on exports [if enabled]
- CTA: Start Free

### Pro ($12/mo, or $9/mo billed yearly)
- 500 image edits/month
- 1,000 copy rewrites/month
- HD exports
- No watermark
- Priority processing
- Batch export (up to 20 per batch)
- History retention (90 days)
- CTA: Upgrade to Pro

### Team ($39/mo, 3 seats included)
- Shared pool: 2,500 edits/month
- Shared templates & history
- Team billing
- Priority email support
- CTA: Contact Sales

### Lifetime (optional, hold for now)
- Suggested if launched: $129 one-time
- Hard cap: 1,500 total edit credits
- Basic support only

## Guardrails
- No “unlimited” claims
- Free tier should show value but prevent abuse
- Reprice if gross margin < 65%
