# Extended FAQ (EN)

## 1) Do I need an account to start?
No. You can try core editing features before creating an account.

## 2) What file types are supported?
JPG, PNG, and WEBP.

## 3) What is the maximum upload size?
10MB per file.

## 4) What counts as one edit?
One successful generated output counts as one edit. Regenerations count separately.

## 5) Is there a free plan?
Yes. The Free plan includes limited daily edits and copy rewrites.

## 6) What do I get with Pro?
Higher monthly limits, HD exports, no watermark, priority processing, and batch export.

## 7) Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes. Cancellation stops renewal. Access continues until the end of the paid period.

## 8) Do you offer refunds?
Please review our Refund Policy for eligibility and timelines.

## 9) Can I use outputs commercially?
Yes, subject to our Terms and content policy.

## 10) Are AI outputs always accurate?
No. AI outputs may vary. Please review before publishing or commercial use.

## 11) Do unused monthly credits roll over?
No. Credits reset each billing cycle.

## 12) Is there an unlimited plan?
No. We use clear usage limits to keep pricing and performance fair.

## 13) How do you handle my data?
See Privacy Policy for collection, usage, retention, and deletion rights.

## 14) Do you use cookies?
Yes. Essential cookies are used for core functionality; analytics cookies may be used where applicable.
